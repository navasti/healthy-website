Diet

Diet is the first thing you should take care of.

Poor nutrition is available for a very large number of diseases. Inappropriate nutrition is the cause of a very large number of diseases while good nutrition can keep you healthy without a lot of physical activity.

What to do?

Become a vegan

The fact is that if you refuse the consumption of animal products along with soy and gluten, it will greatly improve your health.

Activity

Psyhical activity is a great way to improve fitness, lose weight, de-stress and calm down.

What do we recommend to start with?

Hiking

Hiking provides contact with nature and fresh air. This very well affects our mood and harmony. Hiking, like yoga, does not require any equipment and you can start practicing it right away.

Yoga

Yoga does not require any skills and you can start practicing it immediately. It helps to take care of the balance, concentration and gymnastics.

Psyche

We must not forget about a mind. If mind is healthy we have a good attitude and self-esteem and that allows us to do wonderful things. If it's not there's only deconcentration and chaos.

We present the easiest way to start working with your mind.

Meditation

Meditation is a state of relaxation and release from unwanted thoughts. All you need is 5 to 15 minutes a day to feel the difference in managing your own thoughts.
